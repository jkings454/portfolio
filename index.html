About Me

Hi!

My name is Josh Nichols and I'm currently a computer science major attending Salt Lake Community College. I started my journey when I was young, after I decided that I didn't just want to play video games, I wanted to make them. As I taught myself more and more, I realized I wasn't just into video-game design, I was into computers. Since then, I've taught myself a few skills, such as web-design(even a bit of back-end stuff too!), and started making my own websites and programs. I also started to familiarize myself with both computer hardware and software. One of my proudest achievments is building my own PC. I've always loved to learn, and I hope to learn more throughout my life.

This site is my portfolio site. It's meant to be a sort of hub where you can find all of the information you might want to know about me. Hopefully, this will be a place where I can organize all of the great things that I make (and probably some not so great things too). Please, if you have any questions, please, email me at josh.nichols.lives@gmail.com If you're curious about how I made this site, I have a page dedicated to it here.
